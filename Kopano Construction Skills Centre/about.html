About Kopano

Kopano Construction Skills Centre is dedicated to providing accessible and practical training for young people. Our courses are hands-on and community-focused.

To uplift communities by teaching practical construction skills and creating pathways to employment.

To become a leading hub for construction skills training, inspiring youth to build successful careers and contribute to sustainable development in South Africa.

  • Empowerment: Providing opportunities for personal growth and career development.
  • Excellence: Delivering high-quality training and support.
  • Integrity: Promoting honesty, transparency, and ethical practices.
  • Community: Fostering collaboration and giving back to society.

Kopano Construction Skills Centre was founded in 2015 to address the lack of practical training opportunities for youth in construction. Over the years, we have trained hundreds of students who have gone on to successful careers in the building industry.

Through our courses, Kopano has empowered young people, reduced unemployment in local communities, and contributed to building safer, more sustainable homes and structures.
